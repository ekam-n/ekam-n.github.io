Here are the visualizations I made for Assignment 3 of my class, Introduction to Visual Analytics. In this assignment, we were given a dataset tracking sales of videogames. The dataset records certain details about each copy sold of every game. These include: genre of the game, region the copy was sold in, when the copy was sold, what platform the copy was sold for, etc. We were told to make some visualizations to show certain relationships between the data.
